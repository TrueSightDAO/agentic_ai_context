# Store Follow-Up Email Template — Agroverse Ceremonial Cacao

**Read this** when drafting follow-up emails to retail store owners/contacts after leaving ceremonial cacao samples or visiting their store.

**Sender:** All outbound store follow-up emails are sent from **Gary**.

---

## Samples Left During Visits

- **Ceremonial cacao** — from Oscar's Farm (Bahia): https://agroverse.shop/farms/oscar-bahia/index.html
- **Cacao tea** — from Paulo's La do Sitio Farm (Pará): https://agroverse.shop/farms/paulo-la-do-sitio-para/index.html

**Note:** Each farm's cacao has a unique taste profile. We support multiple farms across the Brazilian Amazon.

---

## Standard Elements to Include

1. **Context** — Reference the store visit, who received the samples (e.g. staff name), and that they would pass to the owner
2. **Sample sources** — Link to Oscar's Farm (ceremonial cacao) and Paulo's Farm (cacao tea) as applicable
3. **Offer** — We'd be happy to deliver 5 bags of ceremonial cacao to start with (same arrangement as other stores — consignment basis)
4. **Additional products** — We also offer cacao tea on wholesale
5. **Taste profiles** — Each farm has a unique taste profile; we support multiple farms in the Brazilian Amazon
6. **Mission** — Restore 10,000 hectares of Amazon rainforest; each bag plants a tree
7. **Call to action** — Happy to set up a short call to answer questions and discuss

---

## What NOT to Include

- **Do not mention in-person visits** if you are no longer in the area — offer delivery instead
- Do not promise visits you cannot make

## Example Template

**Subject:** Follow-up – Ceremonial Cacao Samples at [Store Name]

---

Hi [Contact Name],

I hope this finds you well.

I stopped by [Store Name] recently and left some ceremonial cacao samples and cacao tea with [Staff Name]. [She/He] mentioned [she/he] would pass them along to you.

The ceremonial cacao is from Oscar's Farm in Bahia, Brazil—you can read more here: https://agroverse.shop/farms/oscar-bahia/index.html. The cacao tea is from Paulo's La do Sitio Farm in Pará: https://agroverse.shop/farms/paulo-la-do-sitio-para/index.html. Each farm has a unique taste profile, and we support several farms across the Brazilian Amazon.

I wanted to follow up to see if you've had a chance to try them and whether you might be interested in carrying our products. We'd be happy to deliver 5 bags of ceremonial cacao to start with, on the same consignment arrangement we have with other stores. We also offer cacao tea on wholesale.

We work with regenerative cacao farms in the Brazilian Amazon, and each bag sold helps plant a tree as part of our mission to restore 10,000 hectares of rainforest.

I'd be happy to set up a short call to answer any questions and discuss how we could work together.

Best regards,  
Gary
