## 5. The Great Bean Adventure — Supply Chain Board Game

**Duration:** ~28 minutes (first round 18 min + second round free choice 10 min)
**Players:** 15 children simultaneously
**Size:** ~4m × 3m floor map

### 6 Stations:
1. **Opening + "Little Bean Departure Song"** (~2 min) — Assistant demonstrates flow, whole team chants
2. **Bubble Bath Station — Fermentation** (~3 min) — 3 groups smell beans at different stages in parallel
3. **Sunny Beach Station — Drying** (~3 min) — Whole team does the turning motion together
4. **Bean Dash Station — Transport** (~3 min) — Pass and touch burlap sacks + push game pieces
5. **Bean Fork Road — Card Choice** (~4 min) — 3 groups draw cards in parallel (green vs orange-yellow)
6. **Magic Transformation Station — Chocolate Factory** (~3 min) — View process flow cards, award medals

### The Fork Road — Core Design
- **Green cards** (cacao leaf) = Fair Trade / cooperative model: more process, more waiting, but stable farmer incomes
- **Orange-yellow cards** (cacao bean) = commodity / middleman model: faster pace, but price drops and instability
- Non-judgmental — both paths exist in the real world; children discover differences through experience

### Second Round (~10 min)
Free choice: continue the other path, spectate, or rest. Then closing circle sharing.
