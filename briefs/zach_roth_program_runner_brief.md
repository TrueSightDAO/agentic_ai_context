## 2. What the Program Is About

**Event:** Aora × Agroverse Cacao Rainforest Adventure
**Type:** Immersive children's educational event (TEDx-style, Mystic Manor-benchmarked)
**Audience:** 10-15 seed families (parents + children aged 6-12)
**Timing:** Mid-to-late July 2025 (pending three-party confirmation)
**Venue:** Indoor, air-conditioned space in China

**The Core Product:** A configurable, replicable event engine — not a one-off. After cacao, the same skeleton can be used for tea, honey, cotton, and other themes.

**The Narrative:**
- Gary = Guardian of the Cacao Rainforest
- Aora Kuku = the first Aora nature spirit (clue character)
- Children create their own Aora companion online → attend the event → continue the story online after

**The 7 Acts (~90 minutes onsite):**
1. **Prologue** — Identity transformation at Aora Certification Station
2. **Act One** — Rainforest Gate opens, children enter
3. **Act Two** — Sensory Rainforest (4 agroforestry stations: smell, touch, sight, hearing)
4. **Act Three** — Expedition Team Formation (send Aora into the rainforest)
5. **Act Four** — The Great Bean Adventure (supply chain floor board game, ~28 min)
6. **Act Five** — Magic Workshop (make chocolate by hand)
7. **Starlit Campfire** — Gary shares his true story
8. **Epilogue** — Certificates, photos, online continuation reminder

**The Great Bean Adventure Board Game:**
- ~4m × 3m floor map, 15 children playing simultaneously
- 6 stations: fermentation, drying, transport, fork road (Fair Trade choice), chocolate factory
- Green cards = cooperative model; orange-yellow cards = commodity model
- Non-judgmental — children discover differences through experience

**The Cacao Explorer Gift Set:**
- Chocolate mold, Fair Trade cacao starter, exploration tools, Aora character card with QR code
- QR code links to child's latest picture book (online continuation loop)
