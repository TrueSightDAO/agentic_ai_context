## Additional Notes

### Grupo Virgolino de Oliveira (GVO)
Also produces ICUMSA 45 but is currently under **bankruptcy protection** (filed June 2015, $735M bond default). Not recommended for new contracts without careful due diligence.
- Phone: +55 17 3245-9800
- Website: gvo.com.br

### Biosev (Louis Dreyfus)
Was a major ICUMSA 45 producer with 13 mills, but has been **acquired by Raízen**. Assets now integrated into Raízen's operations.

### Pará State
There are **very few sugar mills in Pará** — the Amazon region is not traditional sugarcane territory. The mill Natalia is connected with may be a smaller or newer operation. Most ICUMSA 45 production is concentrated in São Paulo, Minas Gerais, Goiás, Mato Grosso do Sul, and Alagoas.
